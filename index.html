Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from our current LMS?
Zx Designs is built exclusively for enterprise assessments, not course delivery. We focus on speed, analytics, and creating a premium user experience that respects your employees' time, avoiding the clunky interfaces typical of traditional learning systems.
Do you support custom reporting requirements?
Yes. Our platform allows administrators to configure bespoke grading logic and generate branded PDF reports mapped to your organization's specific competency frameworks.
Is the platform secure and compliant?
Security is foundational. We are SOC2 Type II compliant, utilizing end-to-end encryption for all personal data, and offering fine-grained role-based access control (RBAC).
